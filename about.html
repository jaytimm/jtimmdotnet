About

Updated: 2025-08-16

I am a research assistant professor at the University of New Mexico, specializing in linguistics and data science. My research interests include:

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • American Politics and Political Science
  • R Programming and Statistical Analysis
  • Computational Linguistics

I enjoy exploring the intersection of language, technology, and politics through data-driven approaches. This blog serves as a space to share insights, tutorials, and thoughts on these topics.
